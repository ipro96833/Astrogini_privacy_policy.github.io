Last Updated: September 10, 2024

Welcome to AstroGini, your personalized daily guide to positive affirmations and fashion choices aligned with your energy. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you share while using our app.

To enhance your daily experience with affirmations and outfit suggestions, we collect the following personal information:

We use the information we collect to provide personalized daily affirmations and outfit suggestions that match your preferences. Specifically, we use your data for:

  • Limited Astrological Insights: Occasionally, we may provide subtle astrological references to enhance your affirmations and outfit suggestions, though this is not the primary focus of our app.

3. Data Security

Your privacy is important to us. We implement industry-standard encryption methods to securely store and transmit your personal data. Only authorized personnel have access to your data, and we take necessary precautions to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for processing payments or managing data analytics. These services comply with strict privacy and security standards to ensure your information is protected.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as it is necessary to provide our services. If you decide to delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our servers.
